LivingContext pricing combines a **base plan**, **per-seat pricing** on paid plans, and **credits** — the usage currency that agent work consumes. Billing is managed by members with the **Administrator** role.

## Credits

Credits meter agent usage:

- **1 credit = $0.01.**
- Every plan includes a **monthly credit grant** that refreshes each billing cycle.
- On plans that support it, you can **top up** credits at any time through Stripe checkout.

## Plans at a glance

| Plan | Base price | Credits / month | Credit top-ups |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | 500 | — |
| **Starter** | $20 + $20/seat | 2,000 | — |
| **Pro** | $50 + $20/seat | 5,000 | — |
| **Business** | $200 + $20/seat | 20,000 | Yes |
| **Ultra** | $500 + $20/seat | 50,000 | Yes |

## Limits and capabilities

| Plan | Concurrent runs | Triggers | Connectors | Side-effects | Autonomous |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 2 | 1 | 2 | — | — |
| **Starter** | 3 | 5 | 5 | Yes | — |
| **Pro** | 5 | 20 | 10 | Yes | Yes |
| **Business** | 10 | 50 | 25 | Yes | Yes |
| **Ultra** | 20 | 200 | 100 | Yes | Yes |

### What the columns mean

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Concurrent runs">
    How many agent runs can execute at the same time. Additional runs queue until a slot frees up.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Triggers">
    How many triggers you can configure to start agent work automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connectors">
    How many external connections (git providers, Salesforce orgs, and other integrations) your organization can attach.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Side-effects">
    Whether agents may take actions that change external systems — Salesforce writes, deployments, or outbound calls. Side-effecting actions are **always behind human approval gates**, on every plan that allows them. See [Security](/admin/security).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Autonomous">
    Whether agents can be started without a human in the loop: cron schedules, webhook triggers, and the public API. Even autonomous runs still require human approval before any side-effecting action or doc change ships.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Enabling side-effects or autonomous triggers never bypasses review. Every change to your docs passes through the [Inbox](/guides/inbox), a change request, or a PR, and every side-effecting action needs explicit human approval.
</Note>

## Managing your plan

Administrators manage the subscription, seats, and credit top-ups. Top-ups (Business and Ultra) go through Stripe checkout and are added to your balance immediately.

<Tip>
  Watch your credit usage for the first month or two. If agents regularly exhaust the monthly grant, moving up a tier is usually cheaper than constraining how often they run.
</Tip>
